Our Mission

BI Dev Solution exists to help entrepreneurs and teams build resilient foundations, modernize systems responsibly, and transform operational data into decision-ready intelligence.

Whether you’re forming an entity, upgrading a platform, or trying to understand performance across multiple projects, we bring structure, clarity, and measurable outcomes.

Three Service Pillars

Business Foundations

Guidance for Sole Proprietorships, Partnerships, LLCs, and Corporations—with technical setup support like NJ registration readiness, EIN, D‑U‑N‑S profiles, and banking integration preparation.

Digital Solutions

Custom web applications designed, built, deployed, and maintained—plus modernization and support for legacy project code to keep you secure and scalable.

Data Intelligence

Unified analysis across your portfolio and interactive dashboards (Power BI/Tableau) that show your business health, ROI, and operational performance in real time.
